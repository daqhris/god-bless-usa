Poster proofs

Six masters — three formats (OG card, square, A4 print) in two themes (light and dark). Same typographic system: Georgia serif, single accent rule, dates as the second-loudest mark after the title. Local-only audit page (carries noindex).

OG / press card — 1200 × 630

For social cards (Twitter, OpenGraph), press emails, gallery listings. No QR — illegible at thumbnail sizes.

Square — 1080 × 1080

Instagram feed, generic social. Title split across four lines for the format; same hierarchy, more vertical breathing.

Press still — 210 × 297 mm (A4)

Print and PDF press kits. Includes the QR pointing at the visitor URL so a passerby holding the page can scan and listen. Footer carries full credit + licence. Light is the default for printed copies (saves toner); dark is for screens, projectors, and digital press attachments.
