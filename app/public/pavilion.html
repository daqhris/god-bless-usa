Three framed works on the wall of the Solidarity Burundi Pavilion, guest room of the Bosnia and Herzegovina Pavilion. Palazzo Malipiero, Venice.

AI Self-Portrait Technological alien

Textile: embroidery, laser-cutting · 2025

A phyllotaxis design generated by Gemini 2.5 Pro, embroidered on cream fabric, mounted in bamboo wood. The transparent A4 label on the back of the frame reads, verbatim:

GEMINI’S HANDHELD AUTOPORTRAIT — THIS IS NOT A GOOGLE TABLET.

awalkaday 91-2022 Cosmic alien

Digital, blockchain: 2023 · paper, print: 2026

Black-and-white print, 300 × 300 mm, on a black fabric mat cut from leftover Open Design Lab textile. The paper surface reflects flash; the wall-mounted loupe is for inspecting the three lights — A, B, C — arranged like the stars on Burundi’s flag.

Arweave source · Etherscan, token #211

Three types of aliens

Legal. The bulletin re-stages U.S. immigration vocabulary as Catholic liturgy. Text and token in 2024, page in 2025, voice and print in 2026. Bureaucratic time accretes the way visa stamps do.

Technological. The textile renders an AI’s self-portrait in thread. Slow craft hours and machine cycles meet on a single panel — a portrait the model could not produce of itself without a loom.

Cosmic. The photograph was taken on foot in 2022, archived on-chain in 2023, printed in 2026. Three lights in the night sky read only through the loupe — astronomical time framed in pedestrian time.
