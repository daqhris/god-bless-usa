God Bless The United States Of Aliens

An AI-voiced performance, rendered scene by scene. Preview page — not the visitor-facing player.

Headphones recommended. The piece is directed for intimate, non-looping playback through a single visitor's headphones. This preview page plays each scene as a standalone audio file, with a combined master track at the bottom.

Complete Performance

God Bless The United States Of Aliens — Master Track

All thirteen scenes concatenated into a single ~15-minute performance, with a 1.5-second gap between scenes. Produced by npm run render:all-scenes.

Available after you run npm run render:all-scenes locally with your Anthropic API key set. The thirteen per-scene WAVs will populate the sections below at the same time.

Scene I

The Eyewitness Report

Voice: THE EYEWITNESS. Bureaucratic, clipped, filing an incident report from outside the atmosphere.

Scene II

The Chorus (First Appearance)

Voice: THE CHORUS. The congregation fused into a single voice — rendered by layering three female Kokoro voices with small timing offsets.

Scene III

Church Leader's Speech

Voice: THE CHURCH LEADER. Warm authority, pastoral irony.

Scene IV

The Chorus (Second Appearance)

Slightly warmer than the first appearance — the congregation has settled into worship.

Scene V

Church Leader's Speech (continued)

The preacher is now in rhythm. Pacing picks up, then deliberately slows on "loud siren."

Scene VI

The Chorus (Third Appearance)

Fuller now. The congregation responds to the absolution with the refrain.

Scene VII

Church Leader's Speech (continued) + [SILENCE]

The longest unbroken passage. Ends with four full seconds of silence. The visitor must sit inside it.

Scene VIII

Church Leader's Speech (concluded) + [AMEN]

Quiet, almost to oneself. Ends on the only one-word moment of the piece.

Scene IX

The Praying Alien — Chain of Thoughts

Voice: THE PRAYING ALIEN. Interior monologue, drier acoustic, three signals (train / heart pulse / gut feeling).

Scene X

The Chorus (Fourth and Final Appearance)

The alien has rejoined the congregation. The fullest, most inhabited version of the refrain.

Scene XI

Church Leader's Prayer

Prayer, not sermon. Intimate. The preacher speaks toward something above or beyond. Ends on "HUGS".

Scene XII

The Chorus (Final Echo)

Slowest of all four appearances. Each phrase a bell tolling.

Scene XIII

The Blessing

Almost a whisper. Merry Christmas. Happy New Year. Organ drone returns.

Scene XIV (optional)

Coda — The Eyewitness Certification

The filing of incident reports resumes. The ceremony is concluded; the record is sealed. Pass --include-coda to render:all-scenes to include it.
