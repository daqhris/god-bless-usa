God Bless The United States Of Aliens

An AI-voiced performance, rendered scene by scene. Preview page — not the visitor-facing player.

Headphones recommended. The piece is directed for intimate, non-looping playback through a single visitor's headphones. This preview page plays each scene as a standalone audio file.

Scene I

The Eyewitness Report

Voice: THE EYEWITNESS. Bureaucratic, clipped, filing an incident report from outside the atmosphere. Rendered with Kokoro-82M.

Source text: USALIEN — Base blockchain, December 2024, permanently archived on Arweave. Performance by Chris-Armel Iradukunda (daqhris) in collaboration with Claude Opus 4.7 (performance director at runtime, co-developer of this app). Kokoro-82M voice engine © hexgrad, Apache-2.0.

Submission to the Build with Opus 4.7 hackathon (April 2026). Intended for the 61st International Art Exhibition — La Biennale di Venezia, 2026, Bosnia and Herzegovina Pavilion — Domus Diasporica.
