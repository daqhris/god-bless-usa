God Bless The United States Of Aliens

An all-seeing eyewitness report.

Headphones recommended. The piece is approximately eighteen minutes and does not loop. Each visitor initiates their own private mass.

Co-directed by Chris-Armel Iradukunda (daqhris) and Claude Opus 4.7. A guest work in Domus Diasporica by Mladen Bundalo — Pavilion of Bosnia and Herzegovina, 61st Venice Biennale, Palazzo Malipiero, May 9 – November 22, 2026.

AI-voiced performance of the short story "God Bless The United States Of Aliens — An All-Seeing Eyewitness Report", written Christmas Eve 2024 by Chris-Armel Iradukunda (daqhris), minted as the USALIEN ERC-721 on the Base blockchain, archived on Arweave. Co-directed for this performance by daqhris and Claude Opus 4.7; voices by Kokoro-82M (Apache-2.0). A guest work in Domus Diasporica, conceived by Mladen Bundalo, commissioned by Sarita Vujković and curated by Isidora Živković — Pavilion of Bosnia and Herzegovina at the 61st International Art Exhibition (In Minor Keys, Koyo Kouoh), Palazzo Malipiero, May 9 – November 22, 2026. Built during the Built with Opus 4.7 hackathon (Cerebral Valley × Anthropic, April 2026). Open-source, MIT.
