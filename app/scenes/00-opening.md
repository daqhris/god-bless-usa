# Scene 0 — Opening Invocation

*A new opening sequence — not present in the original `god-bless-usa_performance-script.md` PRELUDE, added during the performance direction phase to give the listener a clear first 10–15 seconds. Establishes the work as both surveillance report (alien narrator, file being transmitted) and liturgical event (cathedral bell, organ, the chorus refrain heard once as a premonition before the mass begins).*

---

### CATHEDRAL OPENING

*[Ambient: Roman / Vatican-style bell peal — four tolls at ~1.1 s intervals, alternating between a deeper "mother bell" and a "daughter bell" a major third higher (5:4 ratio — brighter and more celebratory than a minor-third peal). The decays phase through each other; the organ drone enters underneath while the peal is still ringing. Total ~8.5 seconds. Use ambient kind "bell_then_drone".]*

---

### CHORUS — PREMONITION
*[Voice: THE CHORUS. A single hushed line. The listener has not yet heard the refrain in any context — this plants it. Heaviest reverb, slowest speed of any chorus appearance in the piece. Render as a chorus speech segment with speed ≤ 0.85.]*

*🎶 "Someone else..." 🎶*

*[Beat — 2 seconds. The drone breathes alone.]*

---

### EYEWITNESS — REPORT HEADER
*[Voice: THE EYEWITNESS. The typewriter bed begins under the first word and continues through the rest of this scene; it trails into Scene I.]*
*[Tone: bureaucratic introduction, naming the file. The word "Alien" carries the weight — the report is filed by an alien narrator.]*

"Alien Report."

*[Half-beat — 800ms.]*

"December 24, 2024."

*[Pause — 1.5 seconds. Typewriter clusters as the title is being typed.]*

"God Bless The United States Of Aliens."

*[Half-beat — 800ms.]*

"An All-Seeing Eyewitness Report."

*[Pause — 2 seconds. The typewriter trails into the silence that begins Scene I.]*
