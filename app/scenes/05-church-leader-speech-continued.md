### SCENE V — CHURCH LEADER'S SPEECH (continued)
*[Voice: THE CHURCH LEADER]*
*[The preacher is now in rhythm. Pacing picks up slightly into the first long sentence,]*
*[then deliberately slows on "loud siren."]*

"Curious to see if the plot |
of using super-beings as tools, |
not trained at Cambridge University, |
nor fluent in electoral analytics, |
for a thoughtful and successful attempt |
to win human choices |
at a federal competition, |
will end on a poetic note |
or a loud siren."

*[Pause — 1 second.]*

"Some of you |
didn't think of consciously choosing a conciliatory candidate."

*[Pause — 1 second.]*

"Some of you |
defied wise counseling |
and voiced divisive rhetoric."

*[Pause — 2 seconds. The weight of accusation.]*

"Regardless of your careless sins, |
our beloved God |
will always grant you |
as much oxygen |
as your carbon-based embodiments request."
