### SCENE XI — CHURCH LEADER'S PRAYER
*[Voice: THE CHURCH LEADER]*
*[Tone shift: this is prayer, not sermon. More intimate. The preacher is no longer addressing]*
*[the congregation — they are speaking toward something above or beyond.]*
*[Slower than the speech. Every line breathes.]*

"We were all busy watching from home, |
with our heads bent downwards, |
eyeing lit up rectangular screens, |
watching non-Palestinian-flag-waving aliens |
vanishing away |
from a super-weaponry-powered genocidal theatre of combat."

*[Heavy pause — 2.5 seconds. This line must not be softened or accelerated.]*

"Now, |
after jumping forward in the year 2024, |
our peaceful attention |
has become hijacked |
by renewed anxious thoughts."

"Headlines gearing up to announce |
that the world's first powerhouse |
is stunned by the puzzled faces |
of their ground-based defenders!"

*[Pace picks up — the congregation is stirring, the prayer becomes collective panic:]*

"Our brothers and sisters are asking out loud among themselves: |
'What will we do |
to these non-ground-based foreign aliens?'"

*[Each option lands as its own breath — a beat between each:]*

"Arrest?"
*[beat]*
"Shoot?"
*[beat]*
"Threaten?"
*[beat]*
"Coerce?"
*[beat]*
"Embargo?"

*[Pause — 1 second — then the alien citizens search the internet:]*

"All at once, |
USA-based human-looking aliens are googling: |
'What to do when overseen and surveilled |
by beings with bigger balls than yours?'"

"Their red-blooded relatives abroad, |
not attending this church, |
are googling: |
'Why did their sensitive and fenced areas |
get flashed at, |
like in a cul-de-sac film scene, |
as seen from above?'"

"Every one of us wonders: |
'How will they kick away from the USA territory |
the non-state-approved sets of aliens?'"

*[The prayer drops into its most intimate register — smallest, closest:]*

"Dear God, |
wake us up |
when true and untrue aliens |
are NOT counted, triaged, and kicked away from the ballpark |
by technologically superior powerhouses."

*[Pause — 1.5 seconds.]*

"This is NOT one of a series of a mirrored show |
about human-performed drama |
that will enlighten our smiles |
this Christmas season."

*[The preacher turns — a genuine question, not rhetorical:]*

"Are there other kinds of sweetened gifts |
that are not yet deliverable through screens?"

*[Genuine pause — 2 seconds — the preacher waits, as if hoping for an answer.]*

"Guess what's the traditional anxiety-numbing gift |
desired by all diverse-looking beings?"

---

### [ SILENCE ]
*[3 seconds. The drone remains. An answer is forming.]*

---

*[Voice: FULL ENSEMBLE — priest, all four chorus voices, AND the praying alien together. This is the moment of warmth in the entire performance, the equivalent of the congregational handshake / hug / kiss-of-peace. The alien rejoins audibly here. Render as a full_ensemble speech segment with heavy reverb and slow speed. Not loud — collectively quiet, like a held breath shared.]*

**"HUGS."**

*[Pause — 1.5 seconds. Let it be small and real.]*

"Glory to peacekeeping hugs |
in all neighborhoods of the |
United States of Aliens!"
